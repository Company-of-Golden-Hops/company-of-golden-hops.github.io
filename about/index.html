O nama

AURUM ET CERVISIA

Who are we and what do we do?

Company of the golden hops is a Croatian non-profit association of citizens, established for the purpose of studying,promoting and reenecting history from antiquity to the early modern era of Croatian and European cultural and historical heritage.

The association operates according to the most modern and up-to-date world principles, achievements and rules of historical reenectment.

The main theme we are doing is a historical Italian mercenary company from 1375 to 1420.
