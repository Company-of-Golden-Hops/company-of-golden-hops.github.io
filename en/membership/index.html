Membership

How to join the Company ?

For all potential interested members, we recommend reading the page About us and Services.

If you want to participate in the activities and goals of the group, you can apply through the following steps:

Request

The interested person contacts the email of the group and receives the document Request for consideration of membership in the Company of the Golden Hops. They have to fill the document and return it to the group. Interested persons are advised to socialize with members of the group in order to meet as many members as possible.

Voting at the Session of the group and Application Form

If the application is accepted from the interested person, the candidate signs the APPLICATION FORM for membership in the Company of the Golden Hops which they will receive from the secretary of the group. They need to attach their photo and pay the amount of the annual membership fee (200 HRK). They can communicate with the secretary of the group via the email address of the group.

Entry in the register of members

After the complete application from, the secretary logs the new member in the Register of Members. Upon entering the group, the new member is entitled to all information and information resources within the group, as well as rights and obligations under the Codex in the Statute of the group.
